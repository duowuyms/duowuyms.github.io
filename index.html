About me

I am currently the second-year MPhil student in The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHKSZ), supervised by Prof. Fangxin Wang. Before that, I obtained my B.Eng. degree in Jinan University (JNU), where I was supervised by Prof. Lin Cui.

I am also fortunate to work closely with Prof. Zhi Wang at SIGS, Tsinghua University and Prof. Shuguang Cui.

My current research interests include the following topics:

  • Multimedia Networking

  • Video Analytics and Streaming

  • Edge and Cloud Computing

  • Machine Learning

I will graduate from CUHKSZ in 2024 summer and I am actively looking for suitable Ph.D. positions for 2024 fall!
